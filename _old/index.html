Holds your plastic cards

Wally can be used to store your credit cards, loyalty cards and any card with the same size. It turns your iPhone into a digital wallet and makes your fat wallet history.

Wally works offline and requires no registration or login. Just download it, and you are good to go.

Or grab Wally from The App Store

This is the original and first Wally app to be featured on the App Store. It launched in March 2012 and is still evolving.
